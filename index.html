Frequently Asked Questions

A form is a structured way to collect user input. Forms can be used for surveys, registrations, contact information, orders, and much more. With GenZDiv, you can create any type of form without needing to write code.

A form builder is a tool that allows you to create and customize forms easily without any coding knowledge. GenZDiv's form builder provides a drag-and-drop interface, pre-designed templates, and advanced features like conditional logic and payment integration.

A custom form creator lets you build unique forms tailored to your specific data collection needs. Unlike generic form templates, a custom form creator like GenZDiv allows you to design forms that match your brand and include only the fields you need, with your preferred layout and functionality.

Online forms streamline data collection by automatically organizing submissions in a structured format.
