How to Use the Form

Use this interactive Drag & Drop Form to easily compose a formal request for assistance. Simply drag elements such as Name, Address, Reason for Help, Supporting Documents, and Signature into the workspace.

You can edit fields, rearrange them, and preview your final letter before submission.

This tool is perfect for:

  • Submitting requests to authorities or organizations
  • Applying for personal or financial aid
  • Reporting issues or requesting support

Instructions:

  1. Drag elements from the sidebar into the letter area.
  2. Click on any element to edit its content.
  3. Use the preview button to review your letter.
  4. Submit or download the letter when complete.
